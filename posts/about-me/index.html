About Me

Posted on: July 24, 2024 | Reading time: 3 minutes | Word count: 501 words | Author: Dang Duong Minh Nhat

Welcome to my blog! I am Dang Duong Minh Nhat, a passionate mathematics student with a strong interest in cybersecurity. I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science (Honors Program) at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), VNU-HCM, Vietnam. My academic journey has been marked by numerous achievements in core and specialized subjects such as Calculus, Computer Architecture, Data Structures, and Algorithms.

Academic Journey and Achievements

With a deep passion for mathematics and computer science, I have achieved a 4.0 GPA in crucial subjects such as Calculus, Fundamental Programming, and Computer Networks. Before university, I attended the Mathematics Honors Class at Le Hong Phong High School for the Gifted, where I developed strong analytical and logical thinking skills. My efforts have been recognized with various awards, including the National Third Prize in Mathematics in 2021 and multiple medals in international math competitions.

Work Experience

From May to July 2024, I interned at OPSWAT Software Vietnam LLC, where I participated in the OPSWAT Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity Graduate Fellowship Program. This program provided me with hands-on experience in vulnerability identification, exploit development, and defense strategy formulation. Under the guidance of industry experts, I completed the program with a deep understanding of real-world cybersecurity practices.

Previously, I served as a Teaching Assistant for the Discrete Structures course at HCMUT from August to December 2023. In this role, I supported the professor and students in understanding and applying concepts of discrete data structures and graph theory.

Skills and Interests

I am proficient in various programming languages, including C/C++, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I regularly practice competitive programming on platforms such as LeetCode, Codeforces, and HackerRank. Specifically, I have a passion for cryptography and have ranked 94th globally and 12th in Vietnam on CryptoHack.

In addition to my programming and cybersecurity skills, I am an active member of the Bach Khoa Information Security Club (BKISC), where I connect and learn from like-minded individuals.

Awards and Certifications

My efforts have been recognized with numerous awards and certifications. I received the Outstanding Student Award from the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering for the 2022-2023 academic year. Additionally, I secured the 2nd Prize in the International Students’ Olympiad in Cryptography NSUCRYPTO (University’s Team Category) and ranked among the top 7 qualifiers in the Semifinals of the ASEAN Student Contest on Information Security 2023 (ASCIS).

I have also earned certifications from OPSWAT Academy, including Ethical Hacking Expert, WebApp Exploitation Expert, and Password Cracking Expert, as well as training on Critical Infrastructure Protection.

Goals and Vision

My goal is to become a cybersecurity expert capable of assessing and enhancing information system security while contributing to the protection of critical data and personal information. I aim to use my ethical hacking skills to improve online safety and gain a deeper understanding of identifying and mitigating online threats.
