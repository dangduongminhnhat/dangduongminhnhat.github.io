About Me

Posted on: July 24, 2024 | Reading time: 3 minutes | Word count: 535 words | Author: Dang Duong Minh Nhat

Welcome to my blog!

Hello, I’m Dang Duong Minh Nhat, a Computer Science student at HCMUT with a deep passion for cybersecurity, especially cryptography. With a strong foundation in mathematics and cryptographic principles, I am eager to explore and contribute to this field. I created this blog as a space to share my knowledge and connect with others who share the same enthusiasm. I hope my writings will not only provide value to readers but also open new career opportunities for me in cybersecurity and cryptography.
